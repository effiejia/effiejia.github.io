Bon Iver Letterforms

An experimental alphabet by Effie Jia.

About the Project

Date of video recording: 10/17/2019

Location of video recording: Boston TD Garden

Event of video recording: Bon Iver Concert

Song: Naeem

Album: i,i

Letterforms are derived from the changing lightshow during the performance. The letterforms were "found," usually with associated letters from their families (below). Due to the radial nature of the lights, the letters are derived from the centerpoint and then drawn out from the extending rays, ending at the light sources. Any dots represent the straight lines of the origin letter that could not be formed through following the rays.

Family groupings:      AMNUVWZS      BDPR      KXY      EFHILTJ      CGOQ
